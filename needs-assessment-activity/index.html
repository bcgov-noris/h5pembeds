Welcome to Unlocking Educational Needs: Escape Room Challenge

Set in the heart of an ancient library filled with secrets and mysteries, you'll embark on a journey of discovery and planning in the realm of educational program needs analysis.

This escape room adventure is designed to engage you in unravelling the intricacies of needs assessment in program planning while navigating through a series of thought-provoking questions and puzzles.

All you will need to do is collect four keys to escape. Ready to explore?
